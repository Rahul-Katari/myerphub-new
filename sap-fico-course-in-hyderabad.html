What is SAP FICO?

SAP FICO stands for Financial Accounting (FI) and Controlling (CO). It helps businesses manage financial transactions, track expenses, generate reports, and ensure compliance with legal requirements. Professionals trained in SAP FICO can work in various industries, from banking to manufacturing and IT. With companies worldwide adopting SAP solutions, the demand for skilled SAP FICO professionals is at an all-time high.
