About MyERP Hub

Where Thousands Begin Their SAP Career Journey

At MyERP Hub, we empower individuals with industry-relevant SAP skills that unlock high-growth career opportunities. As a leading training and placement provider, we specialize in SAP modules like FICO, MM, SD, and ABAP, combining classroom expertise with real-world application.

Our expert-curated programs and interactive training methods are designed to inspire, engage, and prepare learners—whether you're a graduate, working professional, or resuming your career after a break.

More than just training, MyERP Hub is committed to your success with 100% placement support, ensuring you don't just learn SAP—you launch your career with confidence

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in SAP education

2015 - Foundation

Founded by SAP industry veterans with a vision to create practical, job-oriented training programs. Started with just 2 modules and 3 trainers in a small Noida office.

2017 - Expansion

Expanded to 6 SAP modules and opened second center in Bangalore. Partnered with 15+ companies for placements. Crossed 1,000 students trained milestone.

2020 - Recognition

Awarded "Best SAP Training Institute" by Indian Education Awards. Launched online learning platform during pandemic. Reached 5,000 students trained.

2024 - Today

10,000+ professionals trained with 85% placement rate. 8 centers across India. Recognized as one of India's top 5 SAP training providers.
