List of Companies Using SAP Software

SAP (Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing) is a leading enterprise resource planning (ERP) software solution used by companies to manage business processes such as finance, supply chain, human resources, and customer relationship management. SAP helps organizations integrate their operations, streamline workflows, and enhance data-driven decision-making.
Companies across various industries use SAP to optimize efficiency, reduce costs, and gain a competitive edge. Its customizable modules cater to businesses of all sizes, from small enterprises to multinational corporations.
