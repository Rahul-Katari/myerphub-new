About Trainer

Niranjan Emparala

Mr. Niranjan is the prime faculty and visionary behind our institution, bringing over 13 years of rich expertise in SAP FI/CO. His experience spans across implementation, roll-out, and support projects with both domestic and international clients. He also possesses hands-on experience with SAP S/4 HANA implementations, having worked on-site with renowned organizations.

Throughout his career, Mr. Niranjan has served in key roles such as Functional Lead, Team Lead, and Solution Architect. Currently, he is contributing to a prominent global profile, further solidifying his industry credentials.

One of his standout qualities is his ability to simplify complex topics and present them in an easily understandable manner. His training sessions are highly engaging, often incorporating real-time case studies that make the concepts resonate with every individual. Mr. Niranjan highly values student engagement and encourages questions, offering detailed, patient explanations to ensure thorough understanding.
