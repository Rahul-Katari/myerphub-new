SAP S/4 HANA Course Syllabus Overview

A well-structured SAP S/4 HANA course syllabus in Hyderabad is designed to cover various functional and technical aspects. Below is a breakdown of the key modules:

1. Introduction to SAP S/4 HANA

  • Evolution from SAP ECC to S/4 HANA
  • Key features and benefits
  • SAP Fiori UI and user experience

2. SAP S/4 HANA Architecture

  • HANA database overview
  • SAP S/4 HANA deployment options (On-premise, Cloud, and Hybrid)
  • Key differences between traditional SAP ERP and S/4 HANA

3. Finance & Controlling (SAP FICO)

  • General Ledger Accounting
  • Accounts Payable and Receivable
  • Asset Accounting
  • Controlling and Profitability Analysis

4. Materials Management (SAP MM)

  • Procurement Process
  • Inventory Management
  • Vendor Evaluation and Invoice Verification

5. Sales and Distribution (SAP SD)

  • Order to Cash Process
  • Pricing and Billing
  • Customer Master Data

6. Production Planning (SAP PP)

  • Master Data in Production Planning
  • MRP (Material Requirements Planning)
  • Capacity Planning and Scheduling

7. SAP S/4 HANA Embedded Analytics

  • Real-time reporting and analytics
  • CDS Views and SAP Fiori Apps
  • SAP BW/4 HANA integration

8. SAP S/4 HANA Migration and Implementation

  • System Conversion Process
  • Data Migration Techniques
  • Best Practices for Implementation

9. Career Prospects & Certification Guidance

  • SAP S/4 HANA Certification Process
  • Job Roles and Responsibilities
  • Resume and Interview Preparation
