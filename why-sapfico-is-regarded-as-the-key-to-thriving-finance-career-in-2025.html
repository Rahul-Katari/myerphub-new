What is SAP FICO?

SAP FICO is an imperative module in SAP ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning), specifically designed for the management of financial transactions and accounting information. It holds two key components:

1. SAP FI (Financial Accounting)

This component handles external accounting processes, such as balance sheets, income statements, and financial reporting.

2. SAP CO (Controlling)

This component focuses on internal reporting and cost management, assisting companies plan, thereby controlling their internal processes effectively.
These two components form the backbone of corporate financial management, making SAP FICO a priceless skill for finance professionals. These two components are interdependent.
