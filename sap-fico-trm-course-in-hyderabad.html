What is SAP FICO TRM?

SAP FICO TRM is a critical submodule within SAP’s Treasury and Risk Management suite that focuses on managing financial transactions, risk exposure, and treasury operations. It enables organizations to monitor their liquidity, manage foreign exchange and interest rate risks, and execute efficient cash flow management. With integrated risk management and financial accounting tools, businesses can get real-time insights and stay ahead of any potential financial hurdles.

SAP FICO TRM offers numerous features such as:

Cash and Liquidity Management: Track and manage the organization’s cash flows effectively, ensuring optimal liquidity levels.

Financial Risk Management: Identify, assess, and mitigate financial risks associated with interest rate fluctuations, currency exchange, and commodity prices.

Investment Management: Oversee investments and optimize the return on financial assets.

Hedge Accounting: Manage hedging activities, ensuring compliance with financial regulations and industry standards.
